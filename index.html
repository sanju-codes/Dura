A-ROOF PRODUCT DESCRIPTION



DURA-Roof’s sheet is ASA (Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate) coated PVC roofing sheet.
The products are special 3 layer co-extruded pvc sheets.The top layer material is made with ASA anti-climate engineering resin, which is suitable for outdoor use. Even when exposed to ultra-violet radiation, dampness, heat, chillness and impact, the product retains the colour and other physical properties and has excellent anti-corrosive properties.
