Frequently Asked Questions

Are your UPVC rainwater gutters suitable for all types of properties?

Yes, UPVC gutters are versatile and can be installed on:
  • Residential homes
  • Commercial buildings
  • Agricultural structures
  • Industrial facilities
  • Residential extensions and conservatories
However, the size and type of the products can vary from one property to another. Feel free to contact us to get the right solution for your property.

Why should I choose UPVC gutters over traditional materials?

UPVC gutters offer several advantages:
  • Rust-resistant and corrosion-proof
  • Low maintenance
  • Long-lasting durability
  • Lightweight and easy to install
  • Cost-effective
  • Resistant to weathering and UV damage
  • Do not require painting or frequent repairs

How do I know what gutter size I need?

The right gutter size depends on several factors:
  • Roof area
  • Roof pitch
  • Local rainfall intensity
  • Building height and design
If you want to determine the most appropriate gutter size for your project, just give us a call and provide us with more details.

What's the price of your rainwater gutters?

Our rainwater gutter price varies according to the size of the property you want to install, the type of gutter used and the accessories used. Please contact us to get a quote.

Do you serve outside Kerala?

Of course. Aqua Star serves all over India. So, feel free to get in touch with us no matter where you are located in.

How do UPVC gutters handle heavy rainfall?

Aqua Star UPVC gutters are engineered to:
  • Manage high-volume water flow efficiently
  • Prevent overflow with strategically designed capacities
  • Reduce water splashing through advanced profile designs
  • Withstand local rainfall intensity with robust construction

Is there any other application for rainwater gutters?

Yes! UPVC gutters are perfect for innovative uses like vertical gardening and aquaponics systems. They can be easily repurposed to create efficient water channels for growing plants, routing water in agricultural setups, and designing creative garden installations. Their non-corrosive material, smooth interior, and durability make them ideal for alternative applications beyond traditional roof drainage.
